How to use the Codex

The Naturalist's Codex is built on active recall and spaced repetition. Here is how you can use our platform to maximize your NEET score.

The Study Modes

⚡ Flashcards

Test your memory on individual facts. Flip the card and grade yourself honestly. Our spaced repetition algorithm will ensure you see the cards you struggle with more frequently.

📝 Active Recall

The hardest but most effective mode. You are given a blank slate and must type out or mentally recall the entire concept before revealing the answer. Highly recommended for high-priority chapters.

🧠 Mnemonics

Learn memory tricks and acronyms for complex lists, classifications, and examples. Once you review them here, they will stick with you during the exam.

🧩 Pathways

Drag and drop steps into the correct sequence. Perfect for understanding biological processes, chemical reactions, or life cycles where order is critical.

🎯 Quizzes & Match-the-following

Test your speed and accuracy with standard multiple choice questions and matching matrices, replicating the exact style of the NEET exam.

Priority Tags

Chapters are tagged with a Priority Score (e.g., High Priority · 14%). This percentage represents the historical weightage of this chapter in past NEET exams. Focus your energy on high-priority chapters first.

Experience Points (XP)

As you study, you'll earn XP and level up. Solving difficult pathways and completing active recall sessions grant the most XP. Use this to track your daily consistency!
