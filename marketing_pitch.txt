# Smart Bio Notes - Marketing Overview

## App Features & Value Proposition
- Target Audience: NEET 2027 Aspirants (Medical Entrance Exam in India).
- Subjects Covered: Class 11 & 12 Biology, Physics, and Chemistry.
- Key Selling Points: "Study smarter, not harder."
- Interactive Study Modes: 
  - AI-optimized active recall
  - Flashcards for memorization
  - MCQ Battles and Boss Battles (Gamified learning)
  - Mnemonics for hard-to-remember concepts
  - "Build the Pathway" and "Match-Up" exercises

## Subscription Plans & Pricing (Razorpay)
1. Single Class (Class 11 OR Class 12):
   - First Month Trial: ₹149
   - Recurring Monthly Plan: ₹349/month onwards

2. Both Classes (Class 11 AND Class 12):
   - First Month Trial: ₹289
   - Recurring Monthly Plan: ₹389/month onwards

## Pitch Ideas for Marketing Expert
- Focus on the gamification of NEET prep (MCQ battles, Boss battles).
- Highlight the comprehensive coverage of the NCERT syllabus.
- Emphasize the AI-driven active recall for long-term retention.
- Great value proposition for the Yearly plan (heavy discounts for committed students).

## App Logic & User Flow
- **Authentication System:** Secure email & password signup/login powered by Supabase. Captures Full Name and optional Phone Number during signup.
- **Freemium Model:** Users can sign up for a free account to track their mastery on free chapters.
- **Premium Paywall:** Attempting to access premium chapters triggers an "Unlock Premium" modal.
- **Subscription Checkout:**
  - Users select their Target Class (Class 11, Class 12, or Both).
  - Users select their Billing Cycle (Monthly or Yearly).
  - Support for Coupon Codes to offer promotional discounts.
- **Payment Gateway:** Fully integrated with Razorpay for secure recurring billing.
